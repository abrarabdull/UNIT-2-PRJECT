How To Get Around Saudi

Getting around Saudi is convenient. Major cities including Riyadh and Jeddah have ride-sharing apps such as Uber and Careem. Intercity travel is supported by buses and planes, while the expanding rail network — including the Haramain High-Speed Train — connects key cities efficiently.
